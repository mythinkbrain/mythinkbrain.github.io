Penjelasan:

Cara Membuat Salad Buah Sehat dan Simple

Instruksi:

Pertama, cuci selada dan sayurannya. Biarkan mengering. Kedua, jika selada dan sayuran terlalu besar, sobek kecil-kecil. Selanjutnya, masukkan sayuran hijau ke dalam mangkuk besar untuk mengaduknya. Tambahkan irisan wortel dan mentimun. Anda dapat menambahkan sayuran atau sayuran lain jika Anda mau. Kemudian, kocok 2 sendok makan minyak zaitun dan 2 sendok teh cuka hingga kental dan lembut sebagai balutan salad. Masukkan ½ sendok teh madu bersama dengan minyak zaitun dan cuka. Rasakan. Anda dapat menambahkan lebih banyak jika Anda mau. Masukkan campuran ke dalam mangkuk besar berisi sayuran dan aduk! Setelah itu taburi garam dan merica. Sesuaikan rasanya sesuai kebutuhan. Terakhir, Anda bisa memasukkan campuran lain, seperti keju, agar lebih enak. Salad sehat sekarang siap disajikan!

Demand can be divided into several groups, including requests based on purchasing power and number of supporting subjects.

a. Demand According to Purchasing Power

Based on its purchasing power, demand is divided into three types, namely effective demand, potential demand and absolute demand.

1) Effective demand is a public request for a good or service accompanied by purchasing power or ability to pay. In this type of demand, a consumer really needs the item and he can afford it.

2) Potential demand is a public demand for goods and services that actually have the ability to buy, but have not purchased the goods or services. For example, Mr. Luki actually has enough money to buy a refrigerator, but he doesn't have the desire to buy a refrigerator yet.

3) Absolute demand is consumer demand for a good or service that is not accompanied by purchasing power. In absolute demand, consumers do not have the ability (money) to buy the desired goods. For example, Hendra wants to buy sports shoes. However, Hendra's money is not enough to buy sports shoes. Therefore, Hendra's desire to buy sports shoes could not be fulfilled.

b. Requests according to the number of supporting subjects

Based on the number of supporting subjects, demand consists of individual requests and collective requests.

1) Individual requests

Individual requests are requests made by a person to meet their needs. An example of an individual request form is shown in Table 17.1 regarding the Desi orange request list.

2) Collective demand

Collective demand or market demand is a collection of individual demands or the overall demand of consumers in the market. For example, besides Desi, there are also several other buyers in the market who will buy oranges. If the demands of Desi and her friends are combined, a market demand is formed.
