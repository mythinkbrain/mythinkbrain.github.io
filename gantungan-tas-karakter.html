DINAMIKA

sistem bergerak ke bawah
a = 5 m/s²
g = 10 m/s²
m = 10 kg
T = __?


T = m (g - a)
T = 10 • (10 - 5)
T = 50 N ← jwb


ΣF = m a
m g - T = m a
T = m g - m a
T = m (g - a)diketahui
m= 10 kg
a= 5 m/s²
g= 10 m/s²

ditanya
T=...?

jawab
w= m.g
w= 10 × 10
w= 100 N

w - T= m.a
100 - T= 10 × 5
100 - T= 50
T= 100-50
T= 50 N
