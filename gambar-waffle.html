JAWABAN:

1. What does “her” refer to?

"... who was some six years her senior." The word her refers to Aurelia Maria.

2. What does “their” refer to?

" ... and for a time it seemed as if their career ... ". The word their refers to Aurelia Maria and Williamson Breckinridge Caruthers

3. What does “it” refer to?

" ... and when he recovered from it his face ...". The word it refers to smallpox

4. What does “him” refer to?

"... and give him another trial."  The word him refers to Williamson Breckinridge Caruthers

PEMBAHASAN:

Untuk dapat menjawab soal mengenai reference/ kata rujukan sebenarnya tidak sulit jika kamu sudah menguasai materi tentang pronoun/ kata ganti. Namun kamu juga harus memahami apakah kata-kata tersebut sebagai subject, object, kata ganti milik, keterangan tempat, serta posisinya didalam sebuah kalimat. Untuk itu mari pelajari materi tentang pronoun berikut:

Subject Pronoun

Kata ganti sebagai subject

I => Saya , You => Kamu/ kalian , They => Mereka , We => Kami/ kita , She => Dia (perempuan) , He => Dia (Lelaki) , It => Ini (hewan/benda)

Object Pronoun

Kata ganti sebagai object

Me, You, them, us, her, him, it

Possessive before a noun

Kata ganti kepunyaan setelah kata benda.

my, your, their, our, her, his, its

Possessive after a noun

Kata ganti kepunyaan setelah kata benda

mine, yours, theirs, ours, hers, his, its
