Membuat kalimat simple past dari "bring" dalam bentuk (+), (-) dan (?)


Sebelum qta membuat kalimat, perlu kita ingat bahwa kata kerja yang digunakan dalam simple past adalah bentuk kedua. Dan bentuk kedua dari Bring adalah :

Bring - (BROUGHT) - brought.


Adapun untuk rumus simple past

(+) subject + V2 + Object

(-) subject + did + not + V1 + object

(?) Did + subject + V1 + object


JAWABAN :

(+) He brought my pen yesterday.

(-) He did not bring my pen yesterday.

(?) Did he bring my pen yesterday?

29. Buatlah kalimat aktif dan pasif masing2 dari kata kerja : smoke, send, call, bring, sell


SEND
I sent some messages to my old friend yesterday. -aktf past tense-
some messages were sent to my old friend (by me). -pasif past tense- yg ada d dlm kurung, boleh dipakai, dan boleh dihilangkan.

CALL
Michael called his mother last night.
His mother was called by Michael last night.

BRING
Mr. Frans always brings a stick.
A stick is always brought by Mr. Frans.

SELL
Charlie sold her car yesterday afternoon.
Her car was sold by Charlie yesterday afternoon.

karna gak ada keterangan pake tense apa, jadi itu kalimat yg q pake,, past tense semua, kalo mau diganti dg tense yg lain tinggal disesuaikan aja, diganti kata kerja dan keterangan waktunya.

A cigarette was smoked by him (passive)

A letter was sent by me to my mom (passive)
- she call her nephew (active)
Her nephew was called by her (passive)
- my mom bring a newspaper (Active)

- my uncle sell the glasses (active)
