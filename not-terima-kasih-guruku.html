3. The number of GDP can always tell whether a country is prosperous or not. Do you agree or disagree to the statement? Assalamualaikum para guru, saya minta bantuan untuk soal di atas dan tolong buatkan 4 paragraph. Terima kasih


I do not agree. In my opinion, GDP isn't the most appropriate size to reveal the prosperity of a country. GDP indicates the added value generated by economic units, or it can also be interpreted as the sum of the final values of all goods and services. Judging from the meaning, nothing describes the prosperity of the population on a per capita basis. There are many factors for the high production rate. Beside, income from foreign citizens income are still included in the calculation. It is not impossible if the high value of GDP is not purely from native citizens. So Unlike GNP, which has reduced the income of these foreigners.

Evidence of this can also be found in Indonesia. Indonesia is the fifth highest in Asean. Unfortunately, this prosperity has not yet been felt by all people. The existence of inequality in income distribution is one of the reasons.  With this phenomenon, it may be stated that the country is rich, however now no longer prosperous.

According to BPS, welfare cannot be judged from just one aspect, such as GDP. There are eight aspects that describe prosperity and welfare, namely aspects of Population, Health and Nutrition, Education, Employment, Consumption Levels and Patterns, Housing and Environment, Poverty, and Other Social. However, in order to achieve this, it is necessary for the country's economic growth so that the government can improve other aspects as well.

There is a need for further improvement in the country apart from the economic side, such as GDP. Poverty alleviation, for example. Other aspects also need improvement, such as improving the education system, compulsory education, health facilities, old age insurance, and many more. If these aspects are met, human capital will improve in quality. If all these things can be realized, slowly but surely, GDP will also increase.

Pembahasan

PDB merupakan singkatan dari Produk Domestik Bruto. Menurut BPS, PDB merupakan indikator kondisi ekonomi suatu negara. BPS sebagai badan statistik nasional membagi PDB menjadi dua kategori. Kedua kategori tersebut adalah PDB atas dasar harga berlaku dan PDB atas dasar harga konstan. Keduanya memiliki tujuan yang berbeda implementasinya dalam kebijakan. PDB atas dasar harga berlaku dapat digunakan untuk melihat struktur dan mengetahui ada tidaknya pergeseran ekonomi. Sementara itu, PDB atas dasar harga konstan digunakan untuk mengetahui pertumbuhan ekonomi dari tahun ke tahun. Dari PDB dapat diturunkan indikator ekonomi lainnya seperti

Produk Nasional BrutoProduk Nasional Neto atas dasar harga pasarProduk Nasional Neto atas dasar biaya faktor produksiAngka-angka per kapita
