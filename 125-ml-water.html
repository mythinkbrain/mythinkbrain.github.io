dit : (a) molaritas(M) C2H5OH = ?

(b) molalitas(m) C2H5OH = ?

massa etanol = volume × massa jenis

= 39450 / 2300

= 17,2 M

b). m = M × 1000 / densitas - (Massa Molar terlarut × M)

= 17,2 × 1000 / ( 17,2 × 46) - 0,789

= 17,2 × 1000 / ( 791,2) - 0,789

= 17200/ 790,4
