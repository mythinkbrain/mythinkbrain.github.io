15. Jadi challenge nya, kalian harus melengkapi kalimat tersebut menggunakan clue kata kerja yang ada di dalam kurung ​


Jawaban:

14. Woke, will shine.

15. Have visited.

16. Saw.

17. Became, took, was, have changed.

18. Is really improving, has been watching, studying.

19. Arrived, had prepared.

20. Need to, will be staying.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban:

14. I am sick of rain and bad weather! Hopefully. when we wake up tomorrow morning. the sun will be shining.  

15. I have not traveled much yet: however. I will have visited the Grand Canyon and San Francisco by the time I leave the United States.

16. I had seen many pictures of the pyramids before I went to Egypt. Pictures of the monuments are very misleading. The pyramids are actually quite small.

17. In the last hundred years. traveling has become much easier and very comfortable. In the 19th century. it took two or three months to cross North America by covered wagon. The trip was very rough and often dangerous. Things have changed a great deal in the last hundred and fifty years. Now you can fly from New York to Los Angeles in a matter of hours.

18. Joseph's English is really improving, isn't it? He has watched American television pro-grams and has studied his grammar every day since he first arrived in San Diego. Soon he will be totally fluent.

19. When I arrived home last night. I discovered that Jane had prepared a beautiful candle-lit dinner.

20. If you need to contact me sometime next week. I stay at the Sheraton in San Francisco.  

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan menggunakan bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense, Future Continuous Tense, Future Perfect Tense, Past Perfect Tense, Simple Past Tense, Present Continuous Tense.

Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Future Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Be + Verb-ing + Object

Future Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Have + Verb-3 + Object

Past Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Had + Verb-3 + Object

Simple Past Tense:

Verbal: (+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Object

Semoga membantu ya.
