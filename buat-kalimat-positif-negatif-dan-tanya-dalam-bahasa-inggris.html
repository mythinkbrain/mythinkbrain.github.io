Kalimat tanya positif

Do you have any novels?

Kalimat tanya negatif

Don’t you have any novels?

Penjelasan

Kalimat tanya positif

Formula:
Auxiliary verb + S + Verb + O

Kalimat tanya negatif

Formula:
Auxiliary verb + not + S + verb + O

(–) = negative sentence/kalimat negatif
(?) = interrogative sentence/kalimat tanya

(–) He is not going to buy a bottle of water
