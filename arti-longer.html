10. Verb ke 2 dari longer??


Verb ke 2 dari longer adalah longed.
Maaf kalau salahAnswer: 

The word 'longer' is actually from the word 'long' as its root. 'Long' is an adjective, which means there is no Verb 2 form. 'Longer' is a comparative form of 'long'. In order to make the V2 of it, you need to change it to verb first. The verb of 'long' is 'lengthen'. Thus, the V2 of 'long' (not 'longer') is 'lengthened' as it's a regular verb, which means there's no drastic change of its form.

Mapel: English
