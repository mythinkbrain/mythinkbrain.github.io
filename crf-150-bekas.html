9. 1.50°C=……RFK 2.36°R=……°C°FK 3.14°F=……°CRK 4.225K=……°CRF


1.) 50°C = 4/5 × 50 = 40°R
= (9/5 × 50) + 32 = 122°F
= (5/5 × 50) + 273 = 323 K

2.) 36°R = 5/4 × 36 = 45°C
= (9/4 × 36) + 32 = 113°F
= (5/4 × 36) + 273 = 318 K

3.) 14°F = 5/9 × (14 - 32) = -10°C
= 4/9 × (14 - 32) = -8°R
= {5/9 × (14 - 32)} + 273 = 263 K

4.) 225 K = 5/5 × (225 - 273) = -48°C
= 4/5 × (225 - 273) = -38,4°R
= {9/5 × (225 - 273)} + 32 = -54,4°F
