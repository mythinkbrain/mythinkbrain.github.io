Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

%Keuntungan :

= (H.J - H.B) / H.B × 100%

= Rp.3.900.000 - (Rp.3.000.000 + Rp.300.000) / (Rp.3.000.000 + Rp.300.000) × 100%

= (Rp.3.900.000 - Rp.3.300.000) / Rp.3.300.000 × 100%

= Rp.600.000/Rp.3.300.000 × 100%

= 18,18%

Maka keuntungan yang ia peroleh adalah 18,18%

Overall this is the cheapest and the best computer from Apple for now.

Customer: Would you recommend this product?

Seller: Absolutely, ma'am. Apple always make a great quality product that lasts for years. My Macbook has been there for 5 years and still working great.

Customer: Hmm, okay. So I am taking this one.
