14. 5 soal dialog tentang hope, bertanya dan respon​​​


Jawaban:

Contoh Soal Hope and Wish

1. Contoh soal pilihan ganda wish dan jawabannya

Man: The service is really slow here. I've been trying to get the waiter's attention for the last ten minutes

Woman: You know we have a class at two o'clock

Man:...

Narrator: What is the best response for the man reply?

A. I hope he serves us soon

B. I expect him not to come

C. How come he's so lazy?

D. Hopefully he ignores us

Jawaban: A

Dari percakapan tersebut, dapat diketahui pelayanan di sana sangat lambat. Sedangkan, mereka harus masuk kelas jam 2. Maka respons yang tepat untuk situasi tersebut adalah harapan pelayan dapat melayani mereka sesegera mungkin.

Man: This traffic jam is terrible. I can't even move my car any inch

Woman: Remember, we have an English examination in the next an hour

Man:...

Narrator: What is the best response for the man reply?

A. Hopefully the jam will never end

B. I wish the jam is going to be worse

C. I hope the jam end

D. Don't worry, just let it flow

Jawaban: C

Dari percakapan tersebut diketahui terjadi kemacetan yang parah. Padahal, satu jam lagi mereka harus ujian. Ekspresi yang tepat untuk menanggapi hal tersebut adalah berharap macetnya segera berakhir.

= thanks for your hope

= i hope so

- i hope i can pass the exam

= i hope you will succeed

- i hope you win the competition

- i wish i can buy a new car

= i wish your dream come true

- i wish it was a sunny day

- i wish i had a lot of money

Penjelasan:

Wish adalah digunakan untuk menyatakan keinginan atau harapan yang (mustahil) tidak mungkin bisa terjadi.

Hope digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu harapan atau keinginan yang mungkin masih bisa terjadi dan diwujudkan.

30. contoh kalimat congratulation dan responnya


We congratulate people when we hear good news about someone's achievement, promotion, graduation, etc. The social purpose of congratulating people is to express our happiness about someone's achievement.

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat congratulation dan responsnya yaitu

1. “Congratulations on your wedding. I wish you a happy life ahead.”

Response: “It’s so nice of you to say so.”

2. “Please accept my warmest congratulations on your success.”

Response: “Thank you so much.”

3. “I’d like to congratulate you on the opening of your new store.”

Response: “Thank you, I really appreciate it.”

4. “Congratulations on your new business.”

Response: “Thank you.”

5. “Congratulations on your new position as the manager in your company.”

Response: “Thank you for your kind guidance.”

6. “Good job on your promotion!”

Response: "Thanks!"

7. “Congrats on your wedding.”

Response: “Thanks a lot.”

8. “I saw your performance on the dance show tonight. That’s so fantastic!”

Response: “Thanks! I’m so happy to hear that.”

9. “Congrats on your graduation! You did a good job.”

Response: “Thank you.”

10. “Good job!”

Response: “Thank you.”
