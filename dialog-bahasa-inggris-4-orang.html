7. contoh dialog singkat dalam bahasa inggris 4 orang


Dialog singkat dalam bahasa Inggris 4 orang:

Joni: Hi, Jeni. What do you think about English test today?

Jeni: Frankly, I nailed it.

Rita: Wow, really?

Tina: It was hard, but easy if you prepared for the test.

Jeni: That's right, Tina. English is easy. It's just words, just like Bahasa Indonesia.

Joni: I didn't study last night. I think I'll get bad score.

Jeni: Hey, don't think about the score. Just think about how you can improve your skill in English.

Tina: Jeni is right. English is very useful for us. It's an international language. Even in many countries English is the second language spoken beside their mother tongue.

Rita: But how? The grammar is too difficult.

Jeni: The grammar is easy. Don't just memorize it. You must practice. I mean a lot.

Joni: Well, I think you both have to teach us English.

Rita: Yeah, good idea! Let's practice together!

Pembahasan

Dialog singkat di atas bertema ujian bahasa Inggris. Dalam dialog tersebut ada 4 empat orang yaitu: Joni, Jeni, Rita, dan Tina. Dalam dialog tersebut mereka membicarakan tentang ujian bahasa Inggris yang mereka ikuti. Jeni dan Tina bisa mengerjakannya dengan mudah. Joni dan Rita kemudian meminta bantuan mereka agar mau belajar bahasa Inggris bersama.
