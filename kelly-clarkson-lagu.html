26. Bisa carikan past tense dan present continuous tense dan perfect tense di dalam lirik lagu paper heart-tori kelly?


Present Continuous: S + to be (Am, Is, Are) + Verb invite + O/C

-> Memories are playing in my dull mind

Past Tense: S + V2 + O/C (Positive Form)

S + did not + V1 + O/C (-)

Did + S + V1 + O/C (?)

-> Something in me knew that it was real

Present Perfect: S + has/have + V3 + O/C

-> (Kayanya Gak Ada)
