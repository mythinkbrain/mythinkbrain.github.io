SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

=> MEMBAHAS TENTANG FAKTA KEBENARAN ( GENERAL TRUTH ) , FENOMENA

=> KEBIASAAN YANG DILAKUKAN BERULAN-ULANG ( HABITUAL ACITIVITY/ DAILY ACTIVITY )

=> MENGGUNAKAN KETERANGAN FREKUENSI =

ALWAYS, USUALLY, EVERYDAY, SELDOM, RARELY, OFTEN, TWICE A WEEK, ONCE A DAY

=> POLA/RUMUS

SUBJECT + VERB ( S/ES ) + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT  ( VERBAL SENTENCE )

SUBJECT +TOBE(IS/AM/ARE) + NOUN/ADJECTIVE/ADVERB  ( NOMINAL )

PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

=> MEMBAHAS KEGIATAN YANG DILAKUKAN KETIKA SEDANG DIBICARAKAN SESAAT.

=>MENGGUNAKAN KETERANGAN WAKTU =

RIGHT NOW, NOW, STILL, AT THE MOMENT

=>POLA / RUMUS =

SUBJECT+TOBE(IS/AM/ARE) + VERB-ING + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT

21. 20 contoh kalimat simple present tense positif negatif


Contoh Kalimat Simple Present Tense

Positif:

I always wake up at 5 o'clock in the morning.Shinta usually helps her mother cooking in the kitchen.They are the members of National Football Team.We often walk along the park in the afternoon.Aldo wants to meet Mrs. Susan after school.Dinda plays her piano beautifully.You keep our secret.They work as doctors in that hospital.Anton always spends his money to buy actio figures.Witan usually comes to my house after school.

Negatif:

I don't always wake up at 5 o'clock in the morning.Shinta doesn't help her mother cooking in the kitchen.They are not the members of National Football Team.We don't often walk along the park in the afternoon.Aldo doesn't want to meet Mrs. Susan.Dinda doesn't play her piano beautifully.You don't keep our secret.They don't work as doctors in that hospital.Anton doesn't always spend his money to buy something unnecessary.Witan doesn't come to my house after school.Pembahasan

Daily routines adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan sehari-hari. Kegiatan daily routines ini sering juga disebut sebagai kebiasaan/ habit.

Saat kita ingin menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari/ daily routines, kita perlu menggunakan Simple Present Tense. Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung sehari-hari (Daily routines).

Kata keterangan yang sering digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

• Every day

• Every week

• every month

• every year

• once a week

• twice a day

• three times a month

• three times a year

• always

• often

• usually

• seldom

• rarely

• dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

(+) S + V1 atau S + V1s/es

(-) S + do/ does + V1

(?) Do/ Does + S + V1?

NB:

V1 dan Do untuk subject I, You, They, We

V1s/es dan Does untuk subject He, She, It
