23. change into present continuos tense (+)(-)(?) "mr. bob drives how car every day"​


Jawaban:

(-) mr. bob doesn't drive a car every day

Penjelasan:

Jawaban:

(+) Mr. Bob is driving car everyday.

(-) Mr. Bob isn't driving car everyday.

(?) Is Mr. Bob driving car everyday.

Formula (Remember):

(+) Subject + is/are Verb 1 + -ing.

(-) Subject + isn't/aren't + Verb1 + -ing.

(?) Is/are + subject + Verb 1 + -ing

Example of Verb 1 + -ing:

drive : driving (drive + -ing)

drink : drinking (drink + -ing)

do : doing (do + -ing)

work : working (work + -ing)

25. Ali :...... you do all the test' bob ? bob : Bob yes' I did.​


~ Answer:did

Ali: did you do all the test, Bob?

Bob: yes, I did.

.

~ Discussion:

Halo dan salam kenal. Agar dapat kita melengkapi percakapan Ali di atas, kita perlu memerhatikan dialognya ya.

Mari kita perhatikan pernyataan Bob sebagai petunjuk pertama.

》Bob: Yes, I did.

Adanya kata did merupakan auxiliary verb untuk menunjukkan pekerjaan yang dilakukan di waktu sebelumnya. Memiliki tense berupa simple past.

Ali mengajukan pertanyaan. Sehingga auxiliary verb terletak di awal kalimat. Agar sesuai dengan jawaban Bob, nyatakan dengan auxiliary untuk simple past yaitu did.
