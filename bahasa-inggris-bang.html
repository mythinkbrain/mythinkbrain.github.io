19. bantu bang plis yang jago bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

1. (-) We didn't lend John $200 last night.

(?) Did we land John $200 last night?

2. (+) She drank too much coffee yesterday.

(?) Did she drink too much coffee yesterday?

3. (+) My mother forgot to buy some milk.

(?) Did my mother forget to buy some milk?

4. (-) We didn't choose the steak for dinner.

(?) Did we choose the steak for dinner?

5. (+) The train left at 7 am.

(?) Did the train leave at 7 am?

Semoga membantu (⁠◠⁠‿⁠◕⁠)

Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense:

1. (+) We lent John $200 last night.

(-) We did not lend John $200 last night.

(?) Did we lend John $200 last night?

2. (-) She did not drink too much coffee yesterday.

(?) Did she drink too much coffee yesterday?

(+) She drank too much coffee yesterday.

3. (+) My Mother forgot to buy some milk.

(-) My Mother did not forget to buy some milk.

(?) Did my Mother forget to buy some milk?

4. (+) We chose the steak for dinner.

(-) We did not choose the steak for dinner.

(?) Did we choose the steak for dinner?

5. (+) The train left at 7 a.m.

(-) The train did not leave at 7 a.m.

(?) Did the train leave at 7 a.m.?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?

Semoga membantu ya.
